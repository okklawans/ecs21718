Ocelots are part of the wild cat family and are usually found southwestern USA, Central America, and South America. They are spotted, about 22 to 39 inches long, and have short, tan colored fur. Many confuse the ocelot with a Margay, another member of the cat family, but they have several differences. Like numerous other cats they are most active during twilight and nightime. Lastly, ocelots can be kept as pets! Salvador Dali and others have had this adorable animal as a pet.
